mission

The Churchill Coding Club (CCC) aims to educate students about algorithms and competitive programming. Each meeting, members will spend time learning topics and solving coding problems, both individually and as a team. Members will have the opportunity to participate in coding competitions such as USACO (United States Olympiad in Informatics), ACSL (American Computer Science League), and CF (Codeforces) competitions rounds.
